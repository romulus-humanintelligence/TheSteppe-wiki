    Biomes¶

    The world contains 9 biomes, each with unique terrain, fauna, and flora.

    Pontic Steppe¶

    • Temperature: 1.0x base
    • Moisture: 0.4
    • Base Items: 20 per local map

    Terrain Mix:

    • Grass: 70%
    • Hill: 10%
    • Forest: 10%
    • Sand: 5%
    • Scrub: 5%

    Native Herds:

    • Deer (15%)

    Native Predators:

    • Wolf (25%)

    Trees:

    • Elm (30%)

    Shrubs:

    • Juniper (40%)

    Ground Plants:

    • Feather Grass (30%)
