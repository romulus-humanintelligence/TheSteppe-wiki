    Cultures¶

    Four cultures inhabit the steppe world.

    Scythian¶

    • Lifestyle: Nomadic
    • Settlements: 4-6
    • Preferred Biomes: Pontic Steppe, Forest Steppe

    Camp Names: Ariantas, Kolaxais, Targitaos, Lipoxais, Arpoxais

    Elder Names: Idanthyrsus, Ariapeithes, Scyles, Ateas, Saulius

    Warrior Names: Toxaris, Thamyris, Spargapises, Oricus, Skunkha, Madyes

    Trader Names: Anacharsis, Abaris, Gnuros, Tymnes

    Elder Dialogue
    • "The spirits watch over these lands."
    • "Trade is the blood of the steppe."
    • "Beware the wolves at night."
    Warrior Dialogue
    • "I guard this camp."
    • "The steppe tests all who wander."
    • "Do not bring trouble here."

    Sarmatian¶

    • Lifestyle: Nomadic
    • Settlements: 3-5
    • Preferred Biomes: Central Asian Steppe, Pontic Steppe

    Camp Names: Gatali, Siraces, Aorsi, Roxolani, Iazyges

    Elder Names: Gatalos, Medosakkos, Pharzoios, Inismeos, Rasparagos

    Warrior Names: Saitapharnes, Palakos, Zorsines, Aripharnes, Xarthamos

    Trader Names: Argaitos, Kamasarye, Seuthes

    Elder Dialogue
    • "The iron of the east is strongest."
    • "Our riders fear nothing."
    • "The Sauromatae endure."
    Warrior Dialogue
    • "Our lances pierce all armor."
    • "Strength and honor."
    • "Test us and you will regret it."

    Greek Colony¶

    • Lifestyle: Settled
    • Settlements: 3-4
    • Preferred Biomes: Sea Coast

    Camp Names: Olbia, Panticapaeum, Chersonesus, Tanais, Phanagoria

    Elder Names: Archonides, Paerisades, Leukon, Spartokos, Satyros

    Warrior Names: Diophantus, Mithridates, Herakleides, Nikolaos, Lysimachos

    Trader Names: Hermokrates, Apollodoros, Strabon, Herodoros
