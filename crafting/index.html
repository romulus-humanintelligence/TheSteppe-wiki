    Crafting Overview¶

    The crafting system uses recipes that consume materials and optionally require tools and environment.

    Quality System¶

    Recipes marked with quality roll 3d6 (+ bonus dice from skill). Higher skill = more bonus dice = better quality.

    Roll Quality Durability
    3 Wretched 50%
    4-5 Poor 70%
    6-8 Decent 85%
    9-12 Normal 100%
    13-15 Good 125%
    16-17 Fine 150%
    18 Masterwork 200%

    Skill Bonus Dice¶

    Higher relevant skill grants extra dice (keep best 3):

    • Skill 0-24: +0 dice
    • Skill 25-49: +1 die
    • Skill 50-74: +2 dice
    • Skill 75-99: +3 dice
    • Skill 100: +4 dice

    Environment Requirements¶

    • Near Fire: Must be adjacent to a lit fire
    • Near Water: Must be adjacent to a water tile
    • At Bloomery: Must be adjacent to a built bloomery furnace
