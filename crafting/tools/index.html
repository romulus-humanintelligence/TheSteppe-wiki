    Tool Recipes¶

    Recipes for crafting tools and implements.

    Cordage¶

    Twist plant fibers into strong rope.

    • Time: 20 minutes
    • Output: Cordage (Material, 0.1kg)
    • Trade Value: 2
    • Materials: Plant Fiber x2

    Flint Knife¶

    Knap a flint shard and bind it to a handle.

    • Time: 25 minutes
    • Output: Flint Knife (Weapon, 0.3kg)
    • Trade Value: 4
    • Durability: 20 uses
    • Materials: Flint, Cordage

    Flint Scraper¶

    Shape flint into a wide scraping edge for hides.

    • Time: 25 minutes
    • Output: Flint Scraper (Tool, 0.3kg)
    • Trade Value: 4
    • Durability: 30 uses
    • Materials: Flint, Cordage

    • Output: Bone Needle (Tool, 0.05kg)
    • Trade Value: 3
    • Durability: 40 uses
    • Materials: Large Bone
    • Tools: Any knife

    • Time: 30 minutes
    • Output: Bone Awl (Tool, 0.05kg)
    • Trade Value: 3
    • Durability: 50 uses
    • Materials: Large Bone
    • Tools: Any knife

    • Time: 15 minutes
    • Output: Bone Hook (Tool, 0.1kg)

    • Materials: Large Bone

    • Materials: Hawthorn Thorns x2, Cordage
