    Wolf¶

    The grey brotherhood hunts in the moonless dark. Their song freezes the blood of horses.

    • (same as herd loot via combat)
    • Sinew (30%)

    Bear¶

    A mountain made flesh. Stand your ground and die, or run and die tired.

    • (same as herd loot via combat)
    • Sinew (30%)

    Two-legged wolves with iron teeth. They know the words for mercy but have forgotten the meaning.

    • Bronze Knife
    • Scythian Recurve Bow (rare)
    • Arrows
    • Sinew (30%)

    AI Behavior¶

    • Alert Radius: Distance at which creature notices the player
    • Chase Radius: Maximum pursuit distance (0 = never chases)
    • Aggression: Chance to attack when alerted (0.0-1.0)
    • Flee HP: Creature flees when HP drops to this value (0 = fights to death)
    • Territorial: Will attack if player enters territory radius
    • Pack: Multiple wolves hunt together; alpha leads
