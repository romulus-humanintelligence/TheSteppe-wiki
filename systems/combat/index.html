    Combat System¶

    Turn-based combat with body-part targeting, weapon classes, and wound generation.

    Melee Hit Chance¶

    HitChance = AccuracyBase
          - PainPenalty
          - PartDifficulty
          - ArmPenalty
          - FatiguePenalty
          + AgilityBonus
          + SkillBonus
          + HeightBonus
          + WingspanBonus

HitChance = Clamp(HitChance, 0.05, 0.95)

    Component Breakdown¶

    Component Formula Range
    AccuracyBase Weapon stat 0.60-0.75
    PainPenalty TotalPain × 0.005 0 to ~0.40
    PartDifficulty See table below 0 to 0.35
    ArmPenalty +0.10 right arm, +0.05 left arm wounds 0 to 0.15
    FatiguePenalty (40 - Energy) / 40 × 0.20 if Energy < 40 0 to 0.20
    AgilityBonus (Agility - 10) × 0.01 -0.09 to +0.10
    SkillBonus (Skill - 50) × 0.002 -0.10 to +0.10
    HeightBonus (Height - CultureMean) / 5 × 0.02 variable
    WingspanBonus (Wingspan - Height) / 5 × 0.01 variable

    Body Part Targeting Difficulty¶

    Body Part Penalty
    Torso 0.00
    Left/Right Arm 0.10
    Left/Right Leg 0.10
    Left/Right Foot 0.15
    Head 0.20
    Left/Right Hand 0.20
    Neck 0.25
    Left/Right Eye 0.35

    Ranged Hit Chance¶

    HitChance = BaseAccuracy
          - Falloff × Distance
          - PainPenalty
          - PartDifficulty
          - FatiguePenalty
          - WeatherPenalty / 100
          - WindPenalty

HitChance = Clamp(HitChance, 0.05, 0.95)

    Weather Bow Penalties¶

    Weather Penalty
    Clear / Cloudy / Fog 0%
    Snow -8%
    Rain -10%
    Dust Storm -15%
    Heavy Rain -20%
    Blizzard -25%

    Wind Crosswind Penalty¶

    CrossFactor = |sin(angle between wind and arrow direction)|
WindPenalty = CrossFactor × TierMaxPenalty

TierMaxPenalty: Light 0.05, Moderate 0.15, Strong 0.30

    Archery Skill Compensation

    Archery >= 80: wind penalty halved (× 0.5). Archery >= 60: wind penalty reduced (× 0.7).

    Damage Calculation¶

    RawDamage = Random(MinDamage, MaxDamage + 1)
          + MeleeDamageBonus (melee only)
          × (1 + WingspanBowBonus) (ranged only)

MeleeDamageBonus = floor((Strength - 10) × 0.3)    — range: -3 to +3
WingspanBowBonus = (Wingspan - Height) / 5 × 0.03  — +3% per 5cm wingspan

FinalDamage = Max(1, RawDamage - ArmorDR)

    Quality DR Multiplier Speed Multiplier
    Wretched 0.60x 1.50x
    Poor 0.75x 1.25x
    Decent 0.90x 1.10x

    Unarmed Combat¶

    Move Accuracy Damage Wound Notes
    Punch 0.75 1-4 + weight Bruise (Minor) Fast (8 min)
    Kick 0.65 2-6 + weight Bruise or Sprain (25%) 10 min
    Grapple 0.50 0 — Immobilizes target
    Throw Auto 3-8 + weight Bruise (Moderate) Requires grapple

    Weight Bonus: round((Weight - CultureMeanWeight) / 5) — ±1 damage per 5kg.

    Wound Generation¶

    See Wounds & Healing for full wound mechanics.

    Tribal Provocation¶

    Any successful attack on a tribal NPC with a CampId triggers ProvokeCamp(): all warriors in that camp become hostile, elders and traders flee.

    Skill Advancement¶

    • Melee skill: advances on weapon hit
    • Archery skill: advances on bow hit
    • Unarmed skill: advances on any unarmed non-miss
    • Chance per attempt: (100 - currentSkill) / 200
