    Scent System¶

    Body odor attracts creatures, repels NPCs, and can be managed with scent masks.

    Odor Channels¶

    Three independent channels contribute to total body odor:

    Channel Accumulation Decay
    Base Odor +0.5/hour passive Rain -0.6/hr, Heavy Rain -1.2/hr
    Sweat Per physical action (see below) -1.0/hour
    Blood Combat/looting events -0.2/hour

    Sweat per Action¶

    Action Sweat Added
    Normal movement 0.50
    Sneaking movement 0.25
    Mounted movement 0.15
    Melee/Punch/Kick 1.50
    Ranged/Throw 0.80
    Grapple 2.00 
    TotalRawOdor = Clamp(BaseOdor + SweatLevel + BloodLevel, 0, 100)
EffectiveOdor = Clamp(TotalRawOdor + InventoryOdor + ArmorOdor - TotalMaskReduction, 0, 100)

    Scent Masks¶

    Mask Duration Odor Reduction Action Cost
    Mud 4 hours (240 min) -40 Apply at marsh tile 10 min
    Herb 6 hours (360 min) -30 Rub herbs (consumes 1) 10 min
    Dung 8 hours (480 min) -50 Apply dung (consumes 1) 10 min
    Smoke 30 min (auto-refresh) -20 Stand near lit fire Free

    Masks stack. Maximum total reduction with all 4 active: -140.

    Creature Detection Formula¶

    SCENT_RANGE = 3 × acuity × windDirMult × windStrMult × terrainMult × weatherMult × (odor / 50)

    Detection if player is within SCENT_RANGE tiles (Chebyshev distance).

    Creature Acuities¶

    Creature Acuity
    Wolf 3.0
    Bear 2.5
    Deer 2.0
    Saiga 1.8
    Wild Horse 1.5
    Aurochs 1.2
    Bandit 0.5
    Camp Warrior 0.4
    Camp Trader / Elder 0.2-0.3

    Wind Direction Multiplier¶

    Based on angular difference between scent carry direction and creature position:

    Angle Multiplier
    0° (downwind) 1.0x
    45° 0.5x
    90° (crosswind) 0.25x
    135°+ (upwind) 0.1x
    Calm (no wind) 0.5x

    Wind Strength Multiplier¶

    Wind Multiplier
    Calm 1.0x
    Light 1.5x
    Moderate 2.0x
    Strong 3.0x

    Terrain Multiplier¶

    Terrain Multiplier
    Water / River Bank 0.7x
    Marsh / Dense Forest 0.8x
    Forest 0.9x
    All others 1.0x

    Weather Multiplier¶

    Weather Multiplier
    Rain 0.6x
    Heavy Rain / Blizzard 0.3x
    All others 1.0x

    Item Odor¶

    Inventory items contribute odor (capped at 50 total):

    Item Odor Notes
    Rotten Food 25 Worst
    Raw Fish 20 Scales with freshness
    Raw Meat 15 Scales with freshness
    Raw Hide 12
    Animal Dung 8
    Cooked Meat/Fish 5 Scales with freshness
    Scraped Hide 8
    Tanned Leather 3
    Berries 2
    Healing Herbs 2
    Dried/Preserved 1 Minimal

    Player Smell (Bidirectional)¶

    The player can also smell nearby sources:

    SMELL_RANGE = (SmellStat / 10) × windDirMult × windStrMult × sourceStrength × weatherMult
    Source Strength
    Dead bodies 4.0
    Lit fires 3.0
    Bear 2.5
    Wolf / Camp fires 2.0
    Herds 1.5
    Bandits 0.5

    Checked every 5 game-minutes, max 2 messages per check.

    Trade Penalty¶

    Odor affects trading

    Effective odor > 85: -10% trade discount. Active dung mask while odor > 85: additional -5% penalty.
