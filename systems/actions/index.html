    Actions Reference¶

    Complete list of player actions available through the interaction menu (J key) and direct keybindings.

    Keybindings¶

    Movement¶

    Key Action
    W / Numpad 8 Move North
    A / Numpad 4 Move West
    S / Numpad 2 Move South
    D / Numpad 6 Move East
    Q / Numpad 7 Move Northwest
    E / Numpad 9 Move Northeast
    Z / Numpad 1 Move Southwest
    C / Numpad 3 Move Southeast
    R / Numpad + Rotate clockwise
    F / Numpad - Rotate counter-clockwise
    Space Wait (60 minutes)

    Combat¶

    Key Action
    T Attack (melee or ranged)
    P Punch
    K Kick
    G Grapple
    H Throw (requires grapple)
    B Break free from grapple

    Interface¶

    Key Action
    I Toggle inventory
    J Open interaction menu
    M Toggle map
    V View character sheet
    L View message log
    Escape Pause menu / Back
    X Toggle stealth

    Fire Submenu¶

    Key Action
    1 Build fire
    2 Light fire
    3 Add fuel

    Interaction Menu Actions (J)¶

    Terrain-Based¶

    Action Condition Cost
    Collect Clay Sand near water 20 min
    Collect Stones Rock tiles 20 min
    Strip Bark Forest + tree entity, knife 15 min
    Gather Twigs Forest + tree entity 10 min
    Gather Branches Forest + tree entity 15 min
    Harvest Large Branch Tree entity, axe 20 min
    Fell Tree Tree entity, axe 30 min

    Water Actions¶

    Action Condition Cost
    Fish (Hand) Near water 30 min
    Fish (Spear) Near water + spear 20 min
    Fish (Rod) Near water + rod 40 min
    Fish (Net) Near water + net 60 min
    Refill Container Water/riverbank + empty container 5 min

    Scent Management¶

    Action Condition Cost
    Wash Near water 15 min
    Apply Mud Marsh tile 10 min
    Rub Herbs Has herbs in inventory 10 min
    Apply Dung Has dung in inventory 10 min

    Horse Actions¶

    Action Condition Cost
    Tame Horse Near wild horse, not fleeing 30 min
    Mount Own horse nearby, dismounted 2 min
    Dismount Currently mounted 2 min

    Other¶

    Action Condition Cost
    Examine Tracks Visible tracks nearby 15 min
    Sleep Energy < 90, not in combat Variable
    Craft Via crafting menu Per recipe
