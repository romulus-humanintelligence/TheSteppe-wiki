    Wounds & Healing¶

    Wounds are inflicted during combat. Each has a type, severity, and ongoing effects including bleeding, pain, infection, and healing.

    9 Wound Types¶

    Type Caused By Damage Threshold
    Cut Slash < 8 —
    Gash Slash >= 8 Heavy slash
    Puncture Pierce / Arrow —
    Bruise Blunt < 10 —
    Fracture Blunt >= 10 Heavy impact
    Sprain Unarmed kick (25%) —
    Internal Bleeding — (defined, not currently generated)
    Bite Animal bite —
    Claw Animal claw —

    Severity by Damage¶

    Damage Severity Bleed Rate Pain/min Heal Time
    1-3 Minor 0.01 0.1 300 min (5h)
    4-6 Moderate 0.05 0.3 800 min (13h)
    7-10 Severe 0.15 0.5 2000 min (33h)
    11+ Critical 0.30 0.8 5000 min (83h)

    Bleed Rate Modifiers¶

    Damage Type Multiplier
    Slash / Arrow 1.5x
    Blunt 0.3x
    All others 1.0x

    Infection Rate¶

    Source Rate/min
    Bite 0.02
    Claw 0.015
    Arrow 0.01 (min 0.03 on impact)
    Other 0.005

    Sepsis

    At Infection >= 100: 0.2 HP drain per minute (~12 HP/hour). Lethal without treatment.

    Healing Formula¶

    Wounds heal over time. Rate depends on treatment and condition:

    HealRate = BandagedBonus × EnduranceMult × ProteinMult × FatigueMult × SleepMult
HealTimeRemaining -= HealRate × elapsed_minutes
    Factor Formula Range
    Bandaged 1.0 if bandaged, 0.5 if not 0.5-1.0
    Endurance 0.5 + END × 0.05 0.55-1.50
    Protein 0.4 + protein × 0.006 0.40-1.00
    Fatigue 0.5 + energy/50 × 0.5 (if energy < 50) 0.50-1.00
    Sleep 1.0 + 0.5 × SleepQuality (while sleeping) 1.15-1.75

    Infection blocks healing

    Wounds with Infection >= 50 do not heal until infection is reduced.

    Treatment Actions¶

    Action Cost Effect
    Bandage 30 min Stops bleeding, stops infection growth, doubles heal rate
    Splint 40 min Halves remaining heal time for Fracture/Sprain
    Poultice 30 min Reduces infection, speeds healing (scales with Herbalism)
    Remove Arrow 20 min Removes embedded arrow wound

    Consciousness & Pain¶

    PainThreshold = 80 × (0.5 + Willpower × 0.05)
    Willpower Threshold
    1 44
    10 80
    20 120

    If TotalPain >= PainThreshold, the character falls unconscious.

    Pain decays at 0.01 per minute naturally.

    Movement Penalties from Wounds¶

    Wound Location Cost Increase
    Left Leg +0.30x
    Right Leg +0.30x
    Left Foot +0.15x
    Right Foot +0.15x
    Any Fracture +0.50x each

    Maximum: 3.9x movement cost with all legs/feet fractured.

    Accuracy Penalties from Wounds¶

    Wound Location Accuracy Loss
    Right Arm -0.10
    Left Arm -0.05
    Right Hand -0.15
    Left Hand -0.05

    Maximum: -0.35 accuracy with all arm/hand wounds.

    Eye Damage¶

    EyeHealth = 0.5 × (LeftEyeHP / MaxHP) + 0.5 × (RightEyeHP / MaxHP)
Minimum: 0.1 (never fully blind)

    Affects vision range and facing arc.
