    Horseriding System¶

    Tame wild horses, mount/dismount, and travel at high speed across the steppe.

    Taming¶

    chance = 10 + HorseridingSkill × 0.6
       + Max(0, Perception - 10) × 1.0
       + Max(0, Charisma - 10) × 1.0

if herd is Alert: chance × 0.5
if target is Leader: chance × 0.7
chance = Min(chance, 80%)   — hard cap
    Riding Skill Base Chance With PER 15 + CHA 15
    0 10% 20%
    15 19% 29%
    50 40% 50%
    100 70% 80% (capped)

    Cannot tame fleeing horses. Always advances Horseriding skill.

    Horse Properties¶

    Property Initial Value
    Energy 100
    Bond 5.0
    Base Speed 8.0 mph

    Mounted Speed Formula¶

    speed = 8.0 × skillFactor × energyFactor × bondFactor

skillFactor  = 0.5 + (skill / 100) × 0.7    — 0.5 at 0, 1.2 at 100
energyFactor = 0.5 + (energy / 40) × 0.5     — if energy < 40, else 1.0
bondFactor   = 0.9 + (bond / 100) × 0.2      — 0.9 at 0, 1.1 at 100
    Skill Energy Bond Effective Speed
    0 100 5 3.6 mph
    15 100 20 5.1 mph
    50 100 50 7.1 mph
    100 100 100 10.6 mph

    Horse Energy¶

    State Rate Duration
    Mounted (drain) -0.42/min ~4 hours riding
    Idle (recovery) +0.56/min ~3 hours full recovery

    Low horse energy (< 40) reduces speed by up to 50%.

    Bond¶

    Bond increases while riding at +0.05/min (~3/hour). Max bond: 100. Higher bond grants up to +10% speed bonus.
