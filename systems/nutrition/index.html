    Nutrition System¶

    Three macronutrients tracked independently: Protein, Carbohydrates, and Fat. They affect body composition, healing, fatigue, and warmth.

    Drain Rates¶

    Macro Rate/min Time to Empty Purpose
    Protein 0.00868 ~8 days Muscle maintenance, wound healing
    Carbs 0.03472 ~2 days Energy, fatigue resistance
    Fat 0.00496 ~14 days Warmth, long-term energy

    High physical activity (> 20 actions/min) increases all drain by 1.5x.

    Body Composition¶

    Processed once per game-day based on average macro levels:

    Muscle Mass (0-15 kg)¶

    if avgProtein > 50 AND physicalActions >= 10:
    muscle += 0.02 kg/day
elif avgProtein < 25:
    muscle -= 0.01 kg/day

    Strength Bonus: Clamp(floor(muscleMassKg / 3) - 1, -3, +3)

    Body Fat¶

    avgMacros = (Protein + Carbs + Fat) / 3

if avgMacros > 40:
    surplus = (avgMacros - 40) / 60
    bodyFat += surplus × 0.03 kg/day
else:
    deficit = (40 - avgMacros) / 40
    bodyFat -= deficit × 0.02 kg/day

    Fat clamped by sex: Male 3-35%, Female 10-35% of lean mass.

    Stat Effects¶

    Protein → Healing¶

    HealingMultiplier = 0.4 + protein × 0.006
    Protein Healing
    0 40%
    50 70%
    100 100%

    Carbs → Fatigue Resistance¶

    if carbs < 40: fatigueDrain × (1.0 + (40 - carbs) / 40 × culturePenalty)
    Culture Low-Carb Penalty
    Scythian 1.05x
    Sarmatian 1.05x
    Saka 1.08x
    Greek Colony 1.25x

    Fat → Warmth¶

    if fat < 40: warmthDrain × (1.0 + (40 - fat) / 40 × 0.5)

    At 0 fat: 50% more warmth drain.
