    Fishing System¶

    4 techniques, 5 fish species, biome-specific spawning, and equipment bonuses.

    Techniques¶

    Technique Base Chance Time Min Skill Tool Required
    Hand 10% 30 min 0 None
    Spear 25% 20 min 10 Any spear
    Rod (Angling) 45% 40 min 0 Fishing Rod
    Net 60% 60 min 30 Fishing Net

    Net catches 1-3 fish per successful cast.

    Catch Chance Formula¶

    chance = (basePct + skillBonus + rodBonus) × seasonMod × weatherMod × nightMod
chance = Clamp(chance, 5%, 95%)
    Modifier Value
    Skill bonus +0.4% per Fishing skill point
    Iron rod (MaxUses >= 90) +10%
    Bronze rod (MaxUses >= 65) +5%
    Spring 1.3x
    Summer 1.0x
    Autumn 0.8x
    Winter 0.4x
    Rain / Heavy Rain 0.85x
    Night 0.7x

    Fish Species¶

    Species Weight Rarity Trade Value Biomes
    Perch 0.5 kg 40% weight × 3 All biomes
    Carp 2.0 kg 30% weight × 3 River Valley, Wetland, Forest-Steppe
    Pike 3.0 kg 15% weight × 3 River Valley, Forest-Steppe, Taiga
    Catfish 5.0 kg 10% weight × 3 River Valley, Wetland
    Sturgeon 8.0 kg 5% weight × 3 River Valley only

    Weight varies ±30% per catch. Higher Fishing skill boosts rare species selection.

    Hook Tiers¶

    Hook material affects fishing rod durability:

    Hook Rod Durability
    Bone Hook 1.0x (base)
    Bronze Hook 1.5x
    Iron Hook 2.0x

    Skill Advancement¶

    • Successful catch: always advances Fishing
    • Failed attempt: 50% chance to advance
