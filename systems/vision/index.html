    Vision System¶

    Vision determines what tiles and entities the player can see. It's affected by perception, time of day, weather, wounds, fatigue, and rituals.

    Base Range¶

    BaseRange = 12 × (0.5 + Perception × 0.05)
    Perception Base Range
    1 6 tiles
    10 12 tiles
    15 15 tiles
    20 18 tiles

    Effective Range¶

    EffectiveRange = BaseRange × EyeHealth × NightModifier
               - WeatherReduction
               - FatigueReduction
               - ForestReduction
               + AltitudeBonus
               + TorchBonus
               + RitualBonus

EffectiveRange = Max(2, EffectiveRange)  — always see 2 tiles minimum

    Night Modifier¶

    NightModifier = 1.0 - DarknessLevel × 0.6

    At full night (0.6 darkness): vision reduced to 64% of base.

    Weather Vision Reduction¶

    Weather Tiles Lost
    Clear / Cloudy 0
    Rain -2
    Snow -3
    Heavy Rain / Dust Storm -4
    Fog -5
    Blizzard -6

    Forest Reduction¶

    Terrain Reduction
    Dense Forest -2 tiles
    Forest -1 tile

    Altitude Bonus¶

    Elevation Bonus
    > 0.7 (Highland+) +2 tiles
    > 0.5 (Upland+) +1 tile

    Fatigue Vision Penalty¶

    if energy < 40: penalty = ceil((40 - energy) / 40 × 3)

    Up to -3 tiles at 0 energy.

    Ritual Bonuses¶

    Ritual Vision Bonus Duration
    Vapor Bath +2 tiles 4 hours
    Fly Agaric +3-6 tiles Variable
    Ephedra Tea +1 tile 2 hours

    Facing Arc¶

    EffectiveArc = BaseArcDegrees × Max(0.5, EyeHealth)

    Base arc is ~120°. Always see adjacent tiles (1 tile) regardless of facing.

    Line of Sight¶

    Uses Bresenham ray casting:

    Tile Effect
    Rock / Hill Blocks LOS completely
    Dense Forest Blocks LOS completely
    Forest Partially blocks (checkerboard: (x+y) % 2 == 0)
    All others Transparent

    NPC/Creature Vision¶

    Same formula but with fixed perception values. Stealth reduces effective range by up to 70%. See Stealth.
