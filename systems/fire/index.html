    Fire System¶

    Build, light, and maintain campfires for warmth, cooking, and light.

    Fire Building¶

    Fires need fuel arranged in layers: kindling (twigs), sticks, and logs.

    Fuel Types¶

    Fuel items have BurnMinutes and HeatContribution properties. Adding fuel to an existing fire grants 0.5x the heat contribution.

    Fire Lighting¶

    Chance = 60 (base)
       + 20 (if proper structure: kindling + sticks + logs)
       - 30 (if logs only, no kindling)
       - 10 (if hunger <= 20)
       - 10 (if thirst <= 20)
       + WeatherPenalty
       + WindPenalty

Chance = Clamp(Chance, 5, 95)

    Weather Fire Penalties¶

    Weather Penalty
    Clear / Cloudy / Fog 0
    Dust Storm -5
    Snow -10
    Rain -15
    Heavy Rain -30
    Blizzard Blocked entirely

    Wind Fire Penalties¶

    Wind (effective) Lighting Fuel Burn Extinguish Risk
    Calm 0 1.0x 0%
    Light -5% 1.0x 0%
    Moderate -15% 1.3x 0%
    Strong -30% 1.6x ~5%/hour

    Warmth Radius¶

    WarmthRadius = 1 + floor(Heat / 30)  — range 1-4 tiles

    Warmth restored per minute at distance:

    warmth = 0.5 / (1 + Manhattan_distance)
    Distance Warmth/min
    0 (same tile) 0.50
    1 tile 0.25
    2 tiles 0.17
    3 tiles 0.13

    Torch System¶

    • Equipped in OffHand slot
    • Burns 1 use per game-minute
    • Provides light radius during night (extends vision)
    • Removed from inventory when depleted

    Smoke Mask¶

    Standing near a lit fire (within WarmthRadius) refreshes the smoke scent mask to 30 minutes, providing -20 odor reduction.

    Interactions¶

    • Survival — Fire restores warmth
    • Fatigue — Fire near sleep spot improves quality
    • Scent — Fire grants smoke mask
    • Crafting — Many recipes require "Near Fire"
