    Fatigue & Sleep System¶

    Energy (0-100) is the fourth survival pillar. It drains while awake and recovers during sleep.

    Energy Drain¶

    BaseDrain = 100 / (20 × 60) = 0.0833/min  — 20 hours to deplete
drain = BaseDrain × elapsed / EnduranceMultiplier × CarbsPenalty

EnduranceMultiplier = 0.5 + Endurance × 0.05

    Each physical action costs an additional 0.5 energy.

    Endurance Hours Awake
    1 ~11 hours
    10 ~20 hours
    20 ~30 hours

    Stat Penalties Below 40 Energy¶

    Penalty Formula At 0 Energy
    Movement 1.0 + (40-E)/40 × 0.5 1.5x cost
    Accuracy (40-E)/40 × 0.20 -0.20 accuracy
    Vision ceil((40-E)/40 × 3) -3 tiles
    Healing 0.5 + E/50 × 0.5 0.5x rate

    Collapse¶

    Collapse at 0 Energy

    Forced unconscious for 3-5 hours (random). Recovery at half rate. Wake only when energy reaches 30+.

    Sleep Recovery¶

    RecoveryRate = 0.2083/min × SleepQuality  — base 8h at quality 1.0
    Sleep Quality Full Recovery Time
    0.30 (minimum) ~27 hours
    0.50 (poor) ~16 hours
    1.00 (good) ~8 hours
    1.50 (excellent) ~5.3 hours

    Sleep Quality Factors (12 factors)¶

    Base quality starts at 0.50.

    Factor Bonus Condition
    Tent shelter +0.25 In player tent (CampId = -1)
    Fire (small) +0.15 Near lit fire
    Fire (hot) up to +0.30 Scales with fire heat (0.15 + heat/100)
    Warm (>=60) +0.15 Warmth stat above 60
    Felt Blanket +0.15 Blanket in inventory
    Ground cover +0.10 Hide/Leather/Felt in inventory
    Night time +0.20 20:00-05:00
    Forest terrain +0.05 On Forest/DenseForest tile
    Bare ground -0.15 No tent AND no ground cover
    Cold (<30) -0.30 Warmth below 30
    Bad weather -0.25 Blizzard or Heavy Rain
    Mild bad weather -0.10 Rain, Snow, Dust Storm
    Wind (Moderate) -0.10 After shelter reduction
    Wind (Strong) -0.20 After shelter reduction

    Final quality clamped to [0.3, 1.5].

    Optimal Sleep Setup¶

    Best possible quality: tent + hot fire + warm + blanket + ground cover + night + forest = 0.50 + 0.25 + 0.30 + 0.15 + 0.15 + 0.10 + 0.20 + 0.05 = 1.70 → clamped to 1.50.

    Wake Conditions¶

    Normal sleep: wake if energy >= 100, or target time reached, or HP < 80%, or bleeding > 0.1.

    Forced unconscious: wake ONLY if energy >= 30 or target time reached.

    Interactions¶

    • Wounds — Sleep quality boosts healing (1.15x-1.75x)
    • Nutrition — Low carbs increase energy drain
    • Survival — Exhaustion amplifies warmth drain
    • Vision — Low energy reduces vision range
