    Stealth System¶

    Toggle sneaking to reduce detection range at the cost of movement speed.

    Stealth Toggle¶

    • Key: X
    • Halves movement speed (0.5x)
    • Cancels on entering combat or mounting

    Detection Reduction¶

    stealthReduction = StealthSkill / 200

Terrain bonuses (additive):
+ 0.20  Dense Forest
+ 0.10  Forest
+ 0.05  Scrub

stealthReduction = Min(stealthReduction, 0.70)  — 70% cap

effectiveDetectionRange = Max(2, baseRange × (1 - stealthReduction))
    Skill Base Reduction In Dense Forest
    0 0% 20%
    50 25% 45%
    100 50% 70% (capped)

    AI Detection While Sneaking¶

    Detection Method Normal Sneaking
    Vision range Full Reduced by stealthReduction
    Hearing range 2 tiles 1 tile
    Alert radius Full Halved (min 2)

    Sweat While Sneaking¶

    Sneaking generates half the normal sweat (0.25 vs 0.50 per move), helping stay undetected by scent.

    Skill Advancement¶

    Stealth skill advances when moving while sneaking within 3 tiles of an AI creature.
