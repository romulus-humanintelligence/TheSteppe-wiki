    Trading System¶

    Buy and sell items with camp traders. Prices are modified by reputation, charisma, and odor.

    Price Formula¶

    totalDiscount = reputationDiscount + charismaDiscount - odorPenalty
totalDiscount = Clamp(totalDiscount, -0.15, 0.30)

finalPrice = Max(1, TraderAskValue × (1 - totalDiscount))

    Reputation Discount¶

    reputationDiscount = (CampReputation / 100) × 0.15
    Camp Rep Discount
    0 0%
    50 7.5%
    100 15%

    Charisma Discount¶

    charismaDiscount = Max(0, (Charisma - 10) × 0.01)
    Charisma Discount
    10 0%
    15 5%
    20 10%

    Odor Penalty¶

    Condition Penalty
    Effective odor > 85 -10%
    Dung mask + odor > 85 Additional -5%

    Reputation¶

    • Tracked per camp AND per culture (separate)
    • Each completed trade: +1 to both camp and culture reputation
    • Range: 0-100
    • Discount uses camp reputation (not culture)

    Maximum Discount¶

    Best case: 100 camp rep (15%) + CHA 20 (10%) + no odor = 25% off.

    Worst case: 0 rep + CHA 10 + smelly + dung mask = 15% markup.
